# Store Listing

## Short Description

Forward SMS and RCS messages to your email or another phone number.

## Long Description

Never miss an OTP or verification code sent to a phone you're not carrying.

Auto Relay runs silently in the background and forwards incoming SMS messages to your Gmail account or another phone number — the moment they arrive.

HOW IT WORKS
• Sign in with Google to set your Gmail account as the forwarding destination
• Or enter a phone number to forward via SMS
• Any incoming SMS is immediately relayed to wherever you actually are

WORKS WITH RCS TOO
Auto Relay also listens for Google Messages notifications as a best-effort fallback for RCS messages, so you get coverage beyond SMS.

SMART DEDUPLICATION
When the same message arrives over both SMS and RCS, Auto Relay detects and suppresses the duplicate — you only get one copy.

ACTIVITY LOG
Every received message and forwarding outcome is logged in-app for up to 14 days, so you can always verify what was relayed and when.

FILTER UNKNOWN SENDERS
Optionally hide messages from unknown senders to cut down on noise.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your messages never touch our servers. SMS content is forwarded directly from your device to your own Gmail account via the Gmail API. Nothing is shared with the developer or any third party.

TYPICAL USE CASES
• Secondary phone or SIM that stays at home
• Travel SIM you don't carry daily
• Work phone forwarding to personal email
• Automated workflows that need to read OTPs from an inbox
